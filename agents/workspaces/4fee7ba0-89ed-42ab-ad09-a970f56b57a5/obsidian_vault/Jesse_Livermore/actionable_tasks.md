# Jesse Livermore — Actionable Tasks

> Derived from the strategy of Jesse Livermore in quantitative trading.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Research Jesse Livermore's core trading principles.
- [ ] Learn to identify price action patterns indicating trends.
- [ ] Understand how to interpret volume to confirm price movements.
- [ ] Study different methods for calculating and applying pivot points.
- [ ] Set up a dedicated trading journal for future analysis.
- [ ] Identify and define what constitutes a "pivotal point" for trade entry.
- [ ] Practice identifying the overall market direction on higher timeframes.

## Phase 2 — Build (Week 3-6)
- [ ] Practice identifying strong vs. weak trends using price and volume signals.
- [ ] Apply pivot point analysis to current market data on a demo account.
- [ ] Develop a clear checklist for identifying potential pivotal points for entry.
- [ ] Define specific rules for using a "buffer" around pivot points for entry/exit.
- [ ] Begin paper trading using identified pivotal points and a defined buffer.
- [ ] Document your rationale for each paper trade, including market sentiment.

## Phase 3 — Compound (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Refine your entry and exit criteria based on paper trading performance.
- [ ] Develop a clear, rule-based exit strategy for profitable trades.
- [ ] Implement a strict risk management plan for each trade.
- [ ] Begin small-scale live trading with capital you can afford to lose.
- [ ] Systematically analyze trade performance against the overall market direction.
- [ ] Regularly review your trading journal to identify recurring patterns or mistakes.

## Daily / Weekly Rituals
- [ ] Review the overall market sentiment and major trend direction each morning.
- [ ] Scan your watchlists for potential pivotal points forming on charts.
- [ ] Review all open trades, adjusting stop-losses or profit targets as per plan.
- [ ] Journal all closed trades by end of day, noting rationale and lessons learned.
- [ ] Conduct a weekly performance review of all trades and strategy effectiveness.

## Metrics to Track
| Metric | Target | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Win Rate | >50% | Weekly |
| Average R-Multiple | >1.5 | Weekly |
| Number of Trades | 5-10 | Weekly |
| Trades in Market Trend | >70% | Weekly |
| Pivot Point Accuracy | >60% | Weekly |

# George Soros — Actionable Tasks

> Derived from the strategy of George Soros in quantitative trading.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Research and understand the fundamentals of futures contracts and markets.
- [ ] Study historical market data for chosen commodities or indices.
- [ ] Learn key technical analysis indicators relevant to futures trading.
- [ ] Establish a dedicated trading journal for recording observations and decisions.
- [ ] Define initial risk tolerance and capital allocation rules.
- [ ] Set up a demo trading account with a reputable broker.

## Phase 2 — Build (Week 3-6)
- [ ] Develop a preliminary futures trading strategy based on macro-economic trends.
- [ ] Backtest the developed strategy using historical data for at least 12 months.
- [ ] Execute paper trades in the demo account for at least 3 weeks.
- [ ] Analyze the performance of paper trades, identifying strengths and weaknesses.
- [ ] Refine the trading strategy based on demo account performance.
- [ ] Implement strict position sizing rules for futures contracts.

## Phase 3 — Compound (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Begin live trading with a small, conservative capital allocation.
- [ ] Continuously review and adapt the trading strategy to live market conditions.
- [ ] Scale position sizes incrementally based on consistent profitability.
- [ ] Explore advanced risk management techniques like options hedging.
- [ ] Conduct in-depth post-trade analysis for all live trades.
- [ ] Network with other experienced futures traders to exchange insights.

## Daily / Weekly Rituals
- [ ] Review global economic news and market sentiment each morning before trading.
- [ ] Log all trades, including rationale, outcomes, and emotional state, in the trading journal daily.
- [ ] Conduct a weekly performance review, analyzing overall strategy effectiveness and drawing lessons.

## Metrics to Track
| Metric           | Target   | Frequency |
|------------------|----------|-----------|
| Win Rate         | >55%     | Weekly    |
| Risk/Reward Ratio| >1.5     | Weekly    |
| Max Drawdown     | <10%     | Monthly   |
| Number of Trades | 10-20    | Weekly    |
| Profit Factor    | >1.2     | Monthly   |

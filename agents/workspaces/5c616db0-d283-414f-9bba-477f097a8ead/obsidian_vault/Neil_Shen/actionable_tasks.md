# Neil Shen — Actionable Tasks

> Derived from the strategy of Neil Shen in venture capital.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Research current trends and opportunities within the venture capital landscape, specifically in deep tech.
- [ ] Identify and study 5-7 case studies of companies that achieved successful global expansion from local ecosystems.
- [ ] Define personal long-term investment philosophy focusing on 5, 10, and 20-year horizons.
- [ ] Analyze how 3 leading venture capitalists navigate market uncertainty and document their key strategies.
- [ ] Create a checklist of 7 essential qualities that define great founders, based on industry research.
- [ ] Review personal knowledge gaps in emerging deep tech sectors and prioritize 3-5 areas for focused learning.

## Phase 2 — Build (Week 3-6)
- [ ] Develop a preliminary framework for evaluating deep tech spinouts, considering both technical depth and commercial viability.
- [ ] Map out 3-5 potential local ecosystems globally that offer strategic advantages for new ventures.
- [ ] Practice scenario planning for a chosen industry sector, outlining responses to 3 specific market uncertainties.
- [ ] Schedule informational interviews with 3-5 experienced founders to discuss balancing technical innovation with market demands.
- [ ] Initiate a "curiosity project" to explore an adjacent industry or technology outside current expertise.
- [ ] Formulate a personal 'conviction-building' strategy for making investment decisions in ambiguous situations.

## Phase 3 — Compound (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Present a detailed investment thesis for a deep tech startup, integrating long-term growth and global market potential.
- [ ] Mentor an early-stage founder on strategic planning, emphasizing the balance between product innovation and commercialization.
- [ ] Refine personal investment criteria based on observed market dynamics, founder interactions, and portfolio performance.
- [ ] Actively participate in an international venture capital forum or cross-border investment group.
- [ ] Conduct a quarterly strategic review, adjusting long-term plans based on new market insights and uncertainties.
- [ ] Publish an article or deliver a presentation on the future of venture capital in deep tech or global markets.

## Daily / Weekly Rituals
- [ ] Spend 30 minutes daily reading industry reports, tech news, or macroeconomic analyses.
- [ ] Dedicate 1 hour weekly to exploring a new scientific concept or emerging technology trend.
- [ ] Connect with at least one new contact in the venture capital or tech entrepreneurship ecosystem weekly.
- [ ] Perform a weekly reflection on key decisions made, analyzing their potential long-term implications.
- [ ] Journal weekly about encountered uncertainties and the conviction drivers behind recent strategic choices.

## Metrics to Track
| Metric | Target | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Deep Tech Insights Generated | 3-5 per week | Weekly |
| Investment Conviction Score | 8/10 (self-assessment) | Bi-Weekly |
| Global Market Understanding Score | 7/10 (self-assessment) | Monthly |
| New Meaningful Network Connections | 2 per week | Weekly |
| Hours Spent on Continuous Learning | 5+ hours | Weekly |

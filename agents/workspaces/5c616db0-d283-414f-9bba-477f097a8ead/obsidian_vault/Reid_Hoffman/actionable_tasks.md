# Reid Hoffman — Actionable Tasks

> Derived from the strategy of Reid Hoffman in venture capital.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Research top 10 venture capital firms and their investment theses.
- [ ] Analyze 5 recent successful VC-backed exits, identifying key growth drivers.
 ] Conduct 3 informational interviews with early-stage founders to understand market pain points.
- [ ] Read "Secrets of Sand Hill Road" to grasp fundamental VC deal flow and strategy.
- [ ] Set up a CRM system to track potential deal flow and industry contacts.
- [ ] Define personal investment thesis statement (e.g., "AI in sustainable agriculture").

## Phase 2 — Build (Week 3-6)
- [ ] Network with 10 angel investors and other VCs to explore syndication opportunities.
- [ ] Attend 2 relevant industry pitch events or startup demo days.
- [ ] Evaluate 5 promising startups using a standardized due diligence framework.
- [ ] Develop a concise pitch deck outlining personal investment strategy and focus.
- [ ] Begin drafting initial term sheets for hypothetical investment scenarios.
- [ ] Publish 3 blog posts or LinkedIn articles on emerging market trends.

## Phase 3 — Compound (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Lead or co-lead diligence on 1 actual investment opportunity.
- [ ] Negotiate 1 term sheet, practicing deal structuring and valuation.
- [ ] Secure commitment for 1 syndicate co-investment opportunity.
- [ ] Mentor 2 early-stage founders, providing strategic advice and connections.
- [ ] Publish a thought leadership piece on the future of venture capital in a specific niche.
- [ ] Expand network to include 5 C-suite executives from portfolio-relevant companies.

## Daily / Weekly Rituals
- [ ] Daily: Read tech and VC news (e.g., TechCrunch, Axios Pro) for 30 minutes.
- [ ] Weekly: Review CRM for new contacts, follow-ups, and deal progress.
- [ ] Weekly: Dedicate 1 hour to strategic networking (calls/emails/LinkedIn outreach).
- [ ] Daily: Block 30 minutes for deep work on investment thesis refinement or market analysis.

## Metrics to Track
| Metric | Target | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| New qualified leads | 5/week | Weekly |
| Pitches evaluated | 3/week | Weekly |
| Network connections added | 10/month | Monthly |
| Thought leadership articles published | 1/month | Monthly |
| Capital deployed | $X | Quarterly |

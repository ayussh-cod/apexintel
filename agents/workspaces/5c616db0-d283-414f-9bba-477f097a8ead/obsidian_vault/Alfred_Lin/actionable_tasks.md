# Alfred Lin — Actionable Tasks

> Derived from the strategy of Alfred Lin in venture capital.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Research best practices for hiring for potential, focusing on adaptability and learning ability.
- [ ] Study foundational concepts of first-principles thinking and identify one problem to apply it to.
- [ ] Analyze successful customer-focused strategies from companies like Zappos and Amazon.
- [ ] Define initial growth metrics for a new venture based on early-stage objectives.
- [ ] Identify key characteristics and structures that contribute to building resilient teams.
- [ ] Document lessons from a past personal or professional challenge using a structured framework.

## Phase 2 — Build (Week 3-6)
- [ ] Develop a crisis management framework, including communication protocols and decision-making steps.
- [ ] Implement a weekly team ritual designed to foster an innovative culture, such as a "brainstorming Friday."
- [ ] Brainstorm and outline at least three long-term value creation strategies for a current project.
- [ ] Map out common startup challenges relevant to your field and develop contingency plans for each.
- [ ] Conduct a detailed case study on Airbnb's early growth strategies and how they navigated initial hurdles.
- [ ] Establish a feedback loop mechanism for customers to ensure continuous improvement and focus.

## Phase 3 — Compound (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Review all past challenges encountered, documenting lessons learned and identifying new opportunities.
- [ ] Establish a system for reviewing growth metrics quarterly, focusing on sustainable and disciplined expansion.
- [ ] Mentor new team members on core values, emphasizing long-term vision and purpose.
- [ ] Evaluate the impact of implemented innovation initiatives on team engagement and output.
- [ ] Apply first-principles thinking to a complex strategic problem that has been a long-standing roadblock.
- [ ] Analyze the strategies DoorDash used to scale rapidly while maintaining operational efficiency.

## Daily / Weekly Rituals
- [ ] Daily: Review customer feedback channels (emails, social media, surveys) for new insights.
- [ ] Weekly: Allocate 30 minutes for first-principles problem-solving on a chosen business challenge.
- [ ] Weekly: Conduct a brief "pre-mortem" exercise with the team to identify potential project risks.
- [ ] Daily: Dedicate 15 minutes to reading industry trends or competitor analysis.

## Metrics to Track
| Metric                           | Target               | Frequency   |
|----------------------------------|----------------------|-------------|
| Net Promoter Score (NPS)         | > 60                 | Quarterly   |
| New User Acquisition Cost (CAC)  | < $50                | Monthly     |
| Employee Retention Rate          | > 90%                | Annually    |
| Crisis Resolution Time           | < 24 hours           | As needed   |
| Number of Innovative Ideas Implemented | 5 ideas              | Quarterly   |

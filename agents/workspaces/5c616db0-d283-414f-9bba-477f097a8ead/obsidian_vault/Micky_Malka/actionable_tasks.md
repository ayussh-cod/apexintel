# Micky Malka — Actionable Tasks

> Derived from the strategy of Micky Malka in venture capital.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Research the historical context of major currency devaluations, similar to Venezuela in 1983.
- [ ] Analyze the early financial instincts of successful investors, focusing on their insights before significant market shifts.
- [ ] Identify the core problems that fintech companies like Robinhood, Revolut, and Nubank aimed to solve.
- [ ] Document the initial market conditions and needs that led to the success of prominent fintech startups backed by Ribbit Capital.
- [ ] Develop a clear understanding of "money without context" and its implications in modern finance.
- [ ] Map out the current landscape of emerging fintech trends and potential disruptive technologies.

## Phase 2 — Build (Week 3-6)
- [ ] Formulate a personal investment thesis that prioritizes "depositing ideas" over merely "writing checks."
- [ ] Establish a systematic approach for evaluating fintech startups beyond their immediate financial metrics, focusing on their potential to build robust systems.
- [ ] Create a framework for actively engaging with startup founders to offer strategic guidance and insights.
- [ ] Research specific "systems and practices" that Ribbit Capital helped implement in their portfolio companies.
- [ ] Develop a structured method for identifying companies that align with the "future of finance" vision.
- [ ] Network with founders of early-stage fintech companies to understand their challenges and innovative solutions.

## Phase 3 — Compound (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Review the long-term impact of "deposited ideas" on portfolio companies' growth and resilience.
- [ ] Iterate on personal investment strategies based on the evolving landscape of fintech and global finance.
- [ ] Scale engagement with promising startups by offering more profound strategic partnerships.
- [ ] Conduct in-depth case studies of successful fintech exits from Ribbit Capital's portfolio, e.g., Coinbase or Affirm.
- [ ] Mentor emerging entrepreneurs on the importance of building robust financial systems rather than just products.
- [ ] Explore opportunities to contribute to the broader fintech ecosystem through thought leadership or community building.

## Daily / Weekly Rituals
- [ ] Daily scan of financial news outlets for major fintech developments and regulatory changes.
- [ ] Weekly review of economic indicators, focusing on potential currency volatility or market shifts.
- [ ] Bi-weekly dedicated time for deep research into a specific sub-sector of fintech (e.g., decentralized finance, embedded finance).

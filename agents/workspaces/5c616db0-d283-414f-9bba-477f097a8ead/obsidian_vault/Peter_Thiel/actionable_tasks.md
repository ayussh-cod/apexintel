# Peter Thiel — Actionable Tasks

> Derived from the strategy of Peter Thiel in venture capital.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Identify a specific, underserved small market or niche.
- [ ] Conduct in-depth market research to understand the chosen niche's unique needs.
- [ ] Define the ideal customer profile exclusively for the small market.
- [ ] Develop a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) perfectly tailored for this niche's pain points.
- [ ] Launch the MVP to a highly focused, small group within the niche.
- [ ] Gather intensive qualitative and quantitative feedback from early niche users.
- [ ] Analyze the competitive landscape *within* the selected small niche to find differentiation opportunities.

## Phase 2 — Build (Week 3-6)
- [ ] Iterate rapidly on the MVP based on initial niche user feedback and data.
- [ ] Implement features that significantly deepen product value for core niche users.
- [ ] Develop and execute a marketing strategy designed specifically to dominate the identified niche.
- [ ] Achieve a significant market share (e.g., >50%) within the small niche.
- [ ] Establish strong customer loyalty and retention among the niche user base.
- [ ] Document the successful strategies and lessons learned from achieving niche dominance.
- [ ] Identify the most logical and adjacent market for the next phase of expansion.

## Phase 3 — Compound (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Formulate a detailed strategic expansion plan for the next concentric market.
- [ ] Adapt the product/service to appeal to the new, slightly larger target market.
- [ ] Leverage existing brand reputation and customer loyalty from the initial niche for expansion.
- [ ] Scale operations, sales, and customer support to handle the increased market size effectively.
- [ ] Continuously monitor competitive responses and market dynamics in the expanded territories.
- [ ] Seek out and evaluate further opportunities for concentric market expansion.
- [ ] Invest in R&D to maintain product leadership and competitive advantage across all markets.

## Daily / Weekly Rituals
- [ ] Review daily customer feedback and support tickets from the core niche market.
- [ ] Track weekly progress on niche market share and user engagement metrics.
- [ ] Hold weekly team meetings focused on niche dominance and expansion strategy.
- [ ] Dedicate time each week to brainstorming potential adjacent markets and their requirements.

## Metrics to Track
| Metric | Target | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Niche Market Share | >70% | Weekly |
| Customer Retention (Niche) | >90% | Monthly |
| NPS (Niche) | >60 | Quarterly |
| Expansion Market Adoption Rate | >10% month-on-month | Monthly |
| Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) | <$X (niche specific) | Monthly |

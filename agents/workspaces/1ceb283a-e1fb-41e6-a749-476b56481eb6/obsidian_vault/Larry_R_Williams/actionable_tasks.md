# Larry R. Williams — Actionable Tasks

> Derived from the strategy of Larry R. Williams in trading.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Study the concept of inter-market correlations and their historical impact on asset classes.
- [ ] Identify key correlating assets relevant to your primary trading market (e.g., DXY for crypto).
- [ ] Research and document common long-term chart patterns (e.g., accumulation, distribution) applicable to your chosen assets.
- [ ] Learn to access and interpret the Commitment of Traders (COT) report data for traditional markets (conceptual for crypto).
- [ ] Define initial risk management parameters, including maximum loss per trade and daily stop loss.
- [ ] Set up a dedicated trading journal to meticulously record all observations and potential trade ideas.

## Phase 2 — Build (Week 3-6)
- [ ] Practice identifying inter-market correlation signals on historical charts for chosen assets.
- [ ] Develop a methodology for confirming long-term trend patterns using multiple timeframes.
- [ ] Create a framework for interpreting "smart money" sentiment using proxies for COT data in crypto (e.g., large whale movements, open interest).
- [ ] Formulate precise entry and exit rules based on observed price action and correlation signals.
- [ ] Backtest a minimum of 50 historical trades using your developed strategy, recording results in your journal.
- [ ] Refine entry/exit criteria and risk management based on backtesting performance.

## Phase 3 — Compound (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Implement your refined strategy in a paper trading environment for at least 4 weeks.
- [ ] Analyze paper trading performance weekly, focusing on adherence to rules and psychological discipline.
- [ ] Adjust position sizing and compounding strategy based on consistent paper trading profitability.
- [ ] Develop a routine for regularly reviewing inter-market correlations and adapting to shifting global market dynamics.
- [ ] Create a personal checklist for pre-trade analysis incorporating all four core Williams' techniques.
- [ ] Plan for responsible scaling into live trading once paper trading goals are consistently met.

## Daily / Weekly Rituals
- [ ] Review daily market news and significant economic indicators impacting inter-market correlations.
- [ ] Check primary asset charts for developing long-term patterns (weekly/monthly candle analysis).
- [ ] Analyze "smart money" proxies (e.g., aggregated whale data, funding rates) for sentiment shifts.
- [ ] Scan for potential entry/exit signals using your defined criteria across your watchlist.
- [ ] Update trading journal with new market observations, trade ideas, and personal reflections.

## Metrics to Track
| Metric | Target | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Win Rate | >55% | Weekly |
| Risk/Reward Ratio | >1.5:1 | Per Trade |
| Adherence to Trade Plan | 90% | Weekly |
| Max Drawdown | <10% | Monthly |
| Number of Trades Taken | 5+ | Weekly |

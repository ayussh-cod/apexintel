# Jesse Livermore — Actionable Tasks

> Derived from the strategy of Jesse Livermore in trading.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Learn basic market terminology and order types.
- [ ] Set up a demo trading account on a reputable platform.
- [ ] Read "Reminiscences of a Stock Operator" by Edwin Lefèvre.
- [ ] Define personal risk tolerance and initial capital allocation.
- [ ] Identify 3 potential trading instruments (e.g., specific forex pairs, stocks).
- [ ] Start a trading journal to record market observations.

## Phase 2 — Build (Week 3-6)
- [ ] Develop a simple trading strategy based on support/resistance or moving averages.
- [ ] Backtest the chosen strategy using historical data for at least 3 months.
- [ ] Practice placing simulated trades in the demo account for 20 unique setups.
- [ ] Analyze 50 historical charts to identify recurring patterns.
- [ ] Refine strategy rules based on backtesting and simulated trade results.
- [ ] Establish clear entry, exit, and stop-loss rules for the refined strategy.

## Phase 3 — Compound (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Execute 10 live trades with small, defined risk using the refined strategy.
- [ ] Review each live trade daily, documenting lessons learned in the journal.
- [ ] Calculate weekly win rate, profit factor, and average trade size.
- [ ] Adjust position sizing based on risk management principles (e.g., 1-2% per trade).
- [ ] Explore advanced order types (e.g., trailing stops, limit orders) for optimization.
- [ ] Begin compiling a personal 'playbook' of successful trade setups.

## Daily / Weekly Rituals
- [ ] Review news headlines relevant to chosen trading instruments each morning.
- [ ] Analyze daily charts for potential setups and confirm strategy alignment.
- [ ] Update trading journal with all executed trades and emotional states.
- [ ] Conduct a weekly performance review of all trades, identifying strengths and weaknesses.
- [ ] Spend 30 minutes reading market analysis or trading psychology material.

## Metrics to Track
| Metric | Target | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Win Rate | >50% | Weekly |
| Profit Factor | >1.5 | Weekly |
| Average Risk per Trade | <2% of capital | Per Trade |
| Number of Trades | 5-10 | Weekly |
| Journal Entry Completion | 100% | Daily |

# George Soros — Actionable Tasks

> Derived from the strategy of George Soros in trading.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Research the principles and history of global macro investing strategies.
- [ ] Identify and subscribe to 3 reputable economic news sources focused on global trends.
- [ ] Study historical examples of George Soros's key global macro trades and their catalysts.
- [ ] Learn the fundamentals of options trading, including calls, puts, and implied volatility.
- [ ] Analyze current interest rates, inflation data, and geopolitical events for major economies.
- [ ] Create a preliminary list of tech and healthcare industries to monitor for investment opportunities.

## Phase 2 — Build (Week 3-6)
- [ ] Develop a personal global macro investment thesis based on current economic indicators.
- [ ] Research 5 potential target companies within the identified tech and healthcare sectors (e.g., Horizon Therapeutics, Alphabet, Novo Nordisk).
- [ ] Evaluate the financial health, competitive landscape, and growth prospects of these target companies.
- [ ] Analyze specific call and put options for major ETFs (e.g., BlackRock funds) and trust funds (e.g., Invesco Capital).
- [ ] Define clear entry and exit criteria for potential long-term equity and options trades.
- [ ] Practice options strategy and risk management with a paper trading account.

## Phase 3 — Compound (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Review and adjust the global macro thesis based on new economic data and market shifts.
- [ ] Rebalance portfolio positions to capitalize on confirmed macro trends and minimize downside risk.
- [ ] Conduct quarterly deep-dives into existing holdings for continued relevance and performance against original thesis.
- [ ] Identify 3 new emerging sectors or geographies that align with future global macro predictions.
- [ ] Evaluate the performance of current options positions and roll or close as necessary to maximize returns.
- [ ] Analyze market volatility and implement hedging strategies for existing portfolio holdings.

## Daily / Weekly Rituals
- [ ] Conduct a daily market scan for major news affecting global economies and key sectors.
- [ ] Review portfolio performance and any significant price movements on a weekly basis.
- [ ] Read and summarize weekly economic reports and central bank statements.
- [ ] Check open options positions for profit/loss, time decay, and potential adjustments twice a week.

## Metrics to Track
| Metric | Target | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Portfolio Return (Annualized) | 15% | Quarterly |
| Portfolio Diversification Index | >0.7 (Herfindahl-Hirschman) | Monthly |
| Win Rate on Macro Trades | >60% | Quarterly |
| Maximum Drawdown | <10% | Monthly |

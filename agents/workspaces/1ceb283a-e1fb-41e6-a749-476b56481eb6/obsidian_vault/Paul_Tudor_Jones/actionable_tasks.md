# Paul Tudor Jones — Actionable Tasks

> Derived from the strategy of Paul Tudor Jones in trading.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Research and define your maximum acceptable loss per trade and per day.
- [ ] Set up a trading journal to record all trades, rationale, and outcomes.
- [ ] Learn the fundamentals of technical analysis (e.g., trend lines, support/resistance).
- [ ] Study historical market crashes (e.g., 1929, 1987) to understand market dynamics.
- [ ] Practice identifying major market trends on historical charts without trading.
- [ ] Define your initial trading capital and risk allocation percentage.

## Phase 2 — Build (Week 3-6)
- [ ] Develop a specific trading strategy based on market trends or divergences.
- [ ] Paper trade your chosen strategy for at least 3 weeks, documenting all results.
- [ ] Refine stop-loss and take-profit levels for your chosen instruments.
- [ ] Analyze past paper trades to identify patterns in wins and losses.
- [ ] Implement a position sizing model that aligns with your risk tolerance.
- [ ] Seek out and analyze uncorrelated trading opportunities, mirroring PTJ's approach.

## Phase 3 — Compound (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Begin live trading with a small percentage of capital, strictly adhering to your strategy.
- [ ] Review your live trading performance weekly to identify areas for improvement.
- [ ] Adjust position sizing gradually based on consistent positive performance.
- [ ] Develop routines to manage emotional responses during volatile market periods.
- [ ] Continuously search for market inefficiencies and unique trading ideas.
- [ ] Document deviations from your trading plan and their impact on performance.

## Daily / Weekly Rituals
- [ ] Review overnight market news and major economic releases each morning before trading.
- [ ] Chart key markets and identify potential setups based on your strategy daily.
- [ ] Update trading journal with all trade details, emotions, and lessons learned after market close.
- [ ] Review weekly performance report, identify strengths and weaknesses.
- [ ] Dedicate 30 minutes to studying market history or new trading concepts.

## Metrics to Track
| Metric | Target | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Win Rate | >50% | Weekly |
| Risk/Reward Ratio | >1.5:1 | Per Trade |
| Max Drawdown | <5% | Monthly |
| Number of Trades | 10-20 | Weekly |
| Average Profit/Loss per Trade | Positive | Weekly |
